Akan Cultural Naming

The Akan people of Ghana and Ivory Coast traditionally name their children based on the day of the week they were born.
This naming system is believed to have originated centuries ago and is still practiced by many Akan families today.

In this Web application, the user inputs the date of birth of the newborn as per the input fields;
and depending on the gender of the newborn, the application validates and
alert the Akan name.
